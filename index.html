/ 001 — The bar

Drinks made with the patience they deserve

Nocturne opened in 2017 in a Victorian railway arch in Bermondsey. We serve around forty cocktails, a considered wine list, and a small number of spirits selected without compromise. The menu changes four times a year.

We are not interested in being the loudest bar on the street. We are interested in being the one you come back to. The room is dark, the seats are good, and the bartenders know what they are doing. That is the whole proposition.
